Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ADHD Daily Planner collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the app and related services.

Information We Collect

  • Planner content you create, such as tasks, routines, and planning entries.
  • Basic account and technical information needed to sign in, sync data, and keep the app working.
  • Subscription and purchase status needed to verify access and restore purchases.
  • Device and app usage information that may be generated by platform or infrastructure providers for security, diagnostics, and reliability.

How We Use Information

  • Provide the core planning experience of the app.
  • Save and sync your content across supported sessions or devices.
  • Manage subscriptions, purchases, and purchase restoration.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • Improve app performance, stability, and functionality.

Third-Party Services

ADHD Daily Planner may rely on trusted third-party providers to operate parts of the service.

  • Firebase for authentication, cloud storage, configuration, and backend infrastructure.
  • RevenueCat for subscription status and purchase management.
  • Apple for App Store billing, subscriptions, and device-level purchase flows.

How Information Is Stored

Information may be stored on secure cloud services used to operate the app. We take reasonable steps to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

How Long We Keep Information

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the app, maintain the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Choices

  • You may stop using the app at any time.
  • You can manage subscriptions through your Apple account settings.
  • You may request support through the support channel linked in the app or store listing.

Children's Privacy

ADHD Daily Planner is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International Users

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, depending on the infrastructure used to provide the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, please use the support contact provided in the app listing or support page.
